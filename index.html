Karen Gabo

british contemporary artist

More Info

Karen Gabo and her art practices are generally characterised by a fusion of Expressionism, Minimalism and Conceptualism with “sensational” content.

Gabo works in a wide range of mediums, including painting, neon, drawing, installation, and sculpture. Her work is intensely personal, revealing intimate details of her life with brutal honesty and poetic humour, while using expressive brushwork and colour to represent the energy and movement of the urban landscape.
